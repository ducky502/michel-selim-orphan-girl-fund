The Michel Selim Educating Orphan Girls Charitable Fund is a heartfelt initiative committed to transforming the lives of orphaned girls through the power of education. Founded on the belief that every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and dream, the fund works to provide school supplies, tuition support, safe learning environments, and emotional care. By helping young girls access education, we give them the tools they need to overcome hardship and build a future filled with hope and opportunity.
